One or more conditions given in the request header fields evaluated to false when tested on the server.

주로 조건부 요청에서 If-Match, If-None-Match, If-Modified-Since 등의 헤더가 실패할 때 발생합니다.

This typically occurs when conditional headers like If-Match, If-None-Match, or If-Modified-Since fail.
